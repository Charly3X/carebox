Privacy Policy — Care Box

Last updated: 2025-12-25

Care Box is an offline inventory and expiry-date organizer. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

1. Data We Collect

Care Box does not collect personal data to our servers. The app is designed to work offline.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The information you enter in the app (for example: item name, expiry date, quantity, price, notes, categories, boxes/locations) is stored locally on your device.

3. Device Permissions

Care Box does not require access to your contacts, location, or microphone for its core functionality.

If barcode scanning is added in the future, the app may request camera permission only for scanning purposes.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Care Box does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your data by removing items/boxes inside the app or by uninstalling the app from your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

Care Box is not intended for children under 13.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will reflect any changes.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: charoyan@gmail.com
